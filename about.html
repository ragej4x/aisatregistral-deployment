Asian Institute of Science and Technology

AISAT (Asian Institute of Science and Technology) is a premier educational institution dedicated to providing quality education in the fields of science, technology, and related disciplines. Founded with the vision of nurturing future innovators and leaders, AISAT has been at the forefront of technological education for over a decade.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, quality education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and values necessary to excel in a rapidly evolving global landscape while fostering innovation and responsible citizenship.

Our Vision

To be a leading institution in technological education, recognized for excellence in teaching, research, and community service, producing graduates who are globally competitive and socially responsible.

AISAT CARES

AISAT CARES is our institutional service platform designed to streamline administrative processes and enhance the student experience. Through this platform, we aim to:

  • Facilitate efficient request processing
  • Improve communication between students and administration
  • Provide timely updates on campus activities and announcements
  • Create a more responsive and student-centered environment

Core Values

At AISAT, we are guided by our core values:

  • Excellence - Striving for the highest standards in all endeavors
  • Innovation - Encouraging creativity and forward-thinking approaches
  • Integrity - Upholding ethical standards and accountability
  • Service - Commitment to serving the community and society
  • Inclusivity - Embracing diversity and ensuring equal opportunities
